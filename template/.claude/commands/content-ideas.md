# Content Ideas

Generate a content calendar or list of content ideas for a topic or theme.

## Usage

`/content-ideas [topic or theme]`

Examples:
- `/content-ideas AI adoption for enterprise`
- `/content-ideas fractional executive positioning`

## Process

### 1. Understand the Context
Ask:
- What platforms? (LinkedIn, blog, Twitter, newsletter)
- What's your expertise/unique angle?
- What does your audience care about?
- Any existing content to build on?

### 2. Research (if web search available)
- Trending topics in this space
- Common questions people ask
- Gaps in existing content
- Recent news or developments

### 3. Generate Ideas
Create a mix of:

**Thought Leadership**
- Original perspectives
- Contrarian takes
- Industry predictions

**Educational**
- How-to guides
- Frameworks and models
- Case studies (anonymized)

**Engagement**
- Questions to spark discussion
- Polls
- "Unpopular opinion" posts

**Personal**
- Lessons learned
- Behind-the-scenes
- Career stories

**Timely**
- Reactions to news
- Event tie-ins
- Seasonal relevance

### 4. Organize by Time/Theme
Structure for easy planning.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Mix of content types
- [ ] Range of effort levels
- [ ] Authentic to your voice
- [ ] Audience-focused (not self-promotional)
- [ ] Actionable (can actually create these)

## Integration

If `content/calendar.md` exists:
- Reference existing content
- Avoid repetition
- Build on themes

Offer to update `content/calendar.md` with selected ideas.
