## Status Key
- **Sent** — Initial outreach sent, awaiting response
- **Replied** — They responded, conversation active
- **Meeting Scheduled** — Call/meeting on calendar
- **Following Up** — In follow-up sequence
- **Cold** — No response after sequence, pause
- **Closed** — Won or lost
